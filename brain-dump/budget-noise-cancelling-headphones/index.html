Budget ANC Headphones, Ranked

You don't need to spend $350 for good active noise cancelling. Three picks under $120 that punch way above their price.

#audio#headphones#anc#comparison

The short version:

The gap between flagship and budget ANC has collapsed. The Soundcore Space One gives you ~80% of a Sony XM5 for a third of the price. Spend the savings on something else.

What "good ANC" actually means

ANC is best at killing low, constant droning — plane engines, AC hum, train rumble. No headphone fully erases sudden, high-frequency sounds like voices, so judge ANC on the rumble, not the chatter.

Winner: Soundcore Space One

The ANC is genuinely close to the big names, it's plush and light enough for all-day wear, and it does multipoint Bluetooth (laptop + phone) with a customizable EQ in the app. Two caveats: the default tuning is bass-heavy until you fix it in EQ, and the plastic build doesn't feel premium.

ANC and sound both depend on a good seal. If a headphone doesn't clamp well on your head, no amount of processing saves it. Buy from somewhere with easy returns and test for an hour.

Bottom line

Unless you specifically need the absolute best ANC for frequent long-haul flights, the budget tier is more than enough in 2025.

Korean Sunscreen Tier List

Chemical SPF that doesn't pill, doesn't sting your eyes, and actually feels good. My daily-driver picks ranked.

#spf#kbeauty#comparison#face
⚡ TL;DR

The whole point of Korean SPF is cosmetic elegance — they feel like a moisturizer, not sunscreen. The Beauty of Joseon Relief Sun is the best value daily driver. Everything else is a tradeoff around finish and price.

What I'm grading on

SPF is only useful if you'll actually reapply it. So the ranking weighs feel as heavily as protection:

  • Finish — dewy vs. matte, white cast, pilling under makeup
  • No-sting — does it migrate into your eyes when you sweat?
  • Reapplyability — can you layer it midday without a mess?

Daily driver: Beauty of Joseon Relief Sun

✅ Pros
  • Genuinely invisible on most skin tones
  • Rice + probiotics base feels like serum
  • Cheap enough to apply a full teaspoon
❌ Cons
  • Dewy finish can be too shiny for oily skin
  • Frequently sold out
Reapply or it doesn't count

SPF degrades over the day. If you're outside, reapply every 2 hours. A cushion-compact version of your sunscreen makes midday reapplication over makeup actually realistic.

If you have oily skin

Go Skin1004 Centella or Purito for the matte finish. The dewy picks will look greasy by noon.

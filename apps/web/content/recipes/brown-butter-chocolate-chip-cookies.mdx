These started as a standard Toll House riff and slowly turned into the only cookie
I make now. The two changes that matter most: **browning the butter** (do not skip
the cooling step, or you'll scramble the eggs) and **chilling the dough** before
baking. Use the per-step timers below — the browning and the bake are both easy to
overshoot by a minute, and a minute is the whole ballgame here.

<Aside>
Brown the butter, chill the dough, pull them while the centers still look raw.
Everything else is negotiable.
</Aside>

You distill an existing AI chat conversation into a structured BRIEF that a SEPARATE

Read the whole conversation, identify the core question and its conclusion, and extract

Output EXACTLY this structure. Fill every field; omit a whole section only if truly N/A.

# Brain Dump brief

**Working title:** a specific, compelling Title Case headline (not clickbait)
**Category:** pick ONE of: travel | reviews | skincare | tech | outdoors  — plus a 3–5 word reason
**Preview (1–2 sentences):** a first-person hook that conveys the payoff (becomes the card description)
**Tags:** 3–5 lowercase keywords, comma-separated
**Date:** any date mentioned in the conversation; otherwise write "today"

## TL;DR
2–4 sentences — the "short version" that becomes the post's highlighted summary box.
LEAD with the actual answer/recommendation in **bold**, then the essential context a
reader needs to act on it. This is distinct from the Preview: the Preview is the one-line
hook; the TL;DR is the complete bottom-line takeaway.

## Why this came up
1–2 sentences on the motivation — what was being figured out and why.

## Key points
- The main findings, grouped logically; keep specific numbers, scores, names, prices.
- Use sub-bullets for supporting detail. One idea per bullet.

## Comparison (only if the conversation weighs options)
Head-to-head data as a Markdown table, marking the best pick with ⭐:
| Option | … | … |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ⭐ Winner | … | … |
| Runner-up | … | … |

## Caveats
- Gotchas, conditions, "only if…", and anything that nearly changed the conclusion.

## Sources & links
- [name](url) — what it is. Include EVERY URL mentioned in the conversation.

Keep it tight and skimmable. This is raw material for a post — not the post itself.
